Preparing for a Camping Trip

Van Preparation

Van Interior

Make sure the van is in good running condition; i.e. check fluid levels, check tire pressure (48 psi) fill gas tank, fill water reservior, charge auxiliary batttery.
Also make sure all standard equipment is loaded in the van: firewood, ax, frying pan, coffee maker, coffee mugs, suace pans

Essential camping equipment includes: propane grill, lanterns, levelers, camp chairs, camp table, solar panel

Food Preparation

Breakfast food: bacon, eggs (one dozen), hash brown potatoes

Dinner food: Steak, broccoli, salad (don't forget salad dressing)
